## iOS App Testing

[This link](https://testflight.apple.com/join/8dqGaK6b) will be active if/when Apple approves our app for external testing. Before then, you must do the following:

1. Send Eduard (etanase@purdue.edu) your Apple ID account email to be added to the team.
2. Once you are invited to the team, you must accept the team invite.
3. After you accept the invite, Eduard can add you to the list of app testers, which will send you an email to download the app from TestFlight.
4. Download TestFlight, click the link, and download the app.
5. Follow the guide in how-to.md to test the app.

## Android App Testing

Sophros is currently approved for internal testing on the Google Play Store! In order to test our app, you must do the following:

1. Send Emerson (emaddock@purdue.edu) your Google email tied with your Play Store Account to be added to the tester list.
2. Once you have been added to the tester list, click [this link]( https://play.google.com/apps/internaltest/4701481721545349456) on your phone to access the app download on the Play Store.
3. Follow the guide in how-to.md to test the app.
